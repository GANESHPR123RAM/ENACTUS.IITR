PROJECT KHOJ-

The tourism initiative with the motto Exploring the Unexplored.
In this project, we will be researching
villages or places with the potential to become
widely known as tourist destinations.
Several trips will also be conducted
for the purpose of scouting these places and becoming acquainted
with the various cultures prominent in these unknown regions.
Meanwhile, the economic development of these
unexplored places will also be aimed

PROJECT ARK-

The essence of Ark is developing self-sustainable
local entrepreneurs out of effective upcycling
of floral waste. It aims to employ women
in rural areas to upcycle the floral
waste into useful products . This will reduce
the dumping of waste into water
bodies and prevent their contamination. 3 out
of the 17 SDGs set by the UN are being
targeted and focused upon
in this project. 		                                                                                                                                                                            

PROJECT KAAGAZ-

“Kaagaz” focuses on impacting the environment
by reducing paper wastage and simultaneously
creating jobs to empower the local community.
The core principle behind Kaagaz is the
application of the 3Rs i.e. Reduce, Reuse
and Recycle.
